Got questions? We're here to help

What does Baskal actually do?
You give Baskal a budget, a calorie target, nutrition goals and your supermarket. It returns a complete grocery list — real products at real prices — that satisfies all of those at once.
Which supermarkets are supported?
All the major chains, with live pricing. Pick the one you already shop at and every list is built against its catalogue and prices.
Can it handle diets and allergies?
Yes. Set filters like vegan, vegetarian, gluten-free or specific allergens and Baskal will never put those items in your basket.
How accurate is the budget?
Baskal builds up to your cap and stops. If a swap would push you over, it finds a cheaper item that still hits your nutrition goals.
Do I have to plan meals myself?
No. Baskal starts from your goals, not recipes. You get the groceries; how you cook them is up to you — with plenty of variety built in.
Is there a free version?
You can build your first lists free with no card. Baskal Plus unlocks unlimited rebuilds and every supermarket for £8/month.

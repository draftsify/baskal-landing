FAQ

Questions, answered

What does Baskal actually do?
You set your budget, calorie and nutrition goals and pick your supermarket. Baskal returns the exact grocery list to buy — the right items, in the right amounts, that fit all of it.
Does it work with my supermarket?
Baskal builds lists around real products and prices from major supermarkets. Pick your store when you set your goals and every list is tailored to it.
Can it match my nutrition goals?
Yes. Set a calorie target and macro goals like protein, and Baskal chooses foods and quantities that add up to them across the week.
Will it really stay on budget?
Every item counts toward the total you set, so the list is built to land at or under your budget — before you get to the checkout.
What if I don't like an item?
Swap it. Baskal instantly re-balances the rest of the list so you stay on budget and on target.
How much does it cost?
Baskal is free to start with one goal and one store. Baskal Plus unlocks unlimited lists and every supermarket for a small monthly price.
